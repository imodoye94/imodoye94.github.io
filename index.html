about Bi mí

Bi mí online for Africa is Africa's premiere health information aggregator. A platform providing open access to high quality health information, written by experts in simple language and translated into the local tongue. Health information is available in Text, Audio and Video formats ensuring access for all. Our dream is to empower all Africans to take charge of their health, with priority on our women, children and elderly. Read more...

OUR PRIORITY AREAS

C

Child Health

Since children are unable to make their own health decisions, the caregiver’s decisions are binding on the child and affect his/her health. We at Bi mí are committed to health education and empowerment of the caregiver to make the child healthy, strong and full of life. Out future, are our children, to us at Bi mí online, they are very precious.

M

Maternal Health

Cultural practices such as use of traditional birth attendants, harmful refusal of medical help including caesarean sections, food taboos and cultural fads and all their attendant ills have been well discussed in literature. We at Bi mí are committed to educating women and their spouses, who often wield authority, on safe motherhood and healthy living.

E

Care of the Elderly

Ageism is a leading contributor to morbidity and mortality in the elderly and it makes them often present for healthcare late. In Africa, Ageism is rife, with the belief that disease is normal in old age. We at Bi mí are committed to helping our elders stay healthy.
